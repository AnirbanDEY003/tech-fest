Mohammad irfan

Mohammed Irfan is an Indian playback singer who sings predominantly in Hindi. He has also sung in Tamil, Kannada, Odia, Telugu, Bengali, and Marathi. He won the title of "Jo Jeeta Wohi Super Star 2".He was also one of the contestants of "Amul STAR Voice of India", as well as "Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Challenge 2005", where he was on Ismail Darbar's "Yalgaar Ho Gharana". His song "Dil Sambhal Ja Zara" in the film "Murder 2" made him establish himself as a singer. In 2014 his songs included "Baarish" from "Yaariyan", "Muskurane" from "Citylights", "Banjaara" from "Ek Villain", "Dard Dilo Ke" from The "Xpose", and "Tu Hi Tu" from "Kick".

piyush sharma

Piyush Sharma is an Indian Stand Up Comedian. He is a Civil Engineer from IIT Bombay who turned into an amazing and well-known stand-up comedian who is loved and adored by his audience and fans. He is well known for his amazing stand-up comedy which never fails to get people to laugh their hearts out. Piyush Sharma is known for his presence on Instagram. sunburn

Sunburn, a Percept Intellectual Property, is Asia’s Premiere Electronic Dance Music (EDM) Festival, and is ranked amongst the world’s biggest music festivals. Started in 2007 as a 3 day music festival in Goa, Sunburn has grown to become an aspirational lifestyle brand boasting an eclectic mix of music, entertainment, experiences and celebration that has seeded music tourism in India. Over the past decade, Sunburn has brought together renowned International and Indian artists to entertain hundreds of thousands of dance music lovers across the country and overseas, and positioned India as a prime dance festival destination to the world.
